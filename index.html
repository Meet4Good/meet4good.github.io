Meet 4 Good is a community platform offering regular catchups for Singapore Social Enterprises to take the stage and share their mission, present their actions, and reach out to fellow social entrepreneurs, startups, SMEs and Government sponsors, marketing and PR agencies, investors, corporates, and last but not least volunteers.

Everyone is welcome join our events, play a part, and connect with us on: Eventbrite, Meetup, Facebook, Kuiddle, Unibly
